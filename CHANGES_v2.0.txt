═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🤖 ML PREDICTIONS (Single Supplier Only)
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

When you select a specific supplier, you'll see:

⚠️  DELAY RISK (%)
    • Probability order will be late
    • Based on: historical lead times, order qty, month
    • Red (>60%): High risk of delay
    • Orange (40-60%): Medium risk
    • Green (<40%): Low risk

⚠️  QUALITY RISK (%)
    • Probability defect rate will exceed 5%
    • Based on: historical defects, batch size, category
    • Red (>50%): Schedule incoming inspection
    • Orange (30-50%): Monitor closely
    • Green (<30%): Low defect risk

📅  LEAD TIME CATEGORY
    • Predicted delivery: Early / On-time / Late
    • Based on: urgency, seasonal patterns, location
    • Green: On-time expected
    • Yellow: Early expected
    • Red: Late expected

⚡ PERFORMANCE TIPS
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. For fastest loading, uncheck unused KPIs
   (Fewer charts = faster rendering)

2. Use date range filters to limit data
   (Smaller date range = faster queries)

3. Charts render progressively
   (Wait a moment for all to load)

4. Hover on charts for details
   (No need to click - just hover over bars/lines)

5. Refresh data only when needed
   (Button in left panel)

═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🐛 TROUBLESHOOTING
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CHARTS STILL NOT SHOWING:
  1. Refresh browser (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  2. Check that checklist has items selected
  3. Check browser console for errors (F12)
  4. Restart app: Stop (Ctrl+C) and run again

NO DATA IN CHARTS:
  1. Check date range - ensure it has data
  2. Verify supplier selection
  3. Check Status filter - try "All"
  4. Click "Refresh Data" button

ML PREDICTIONS NOT SHOWING:
  1. Make sure you selected a specific supplier
  2. (They only show for individual suppliers, not "All")
  3. Wait 2-3 seconds for calculations

SLOW PERFORMANCE:
  1. Uncheck unnecessary KPIs
  2. Use narrower date range
  3. Reduce number of filters
  4. Restart the app
